# claude-skills

- **[qa-loop](qa-loop/SKILL.MD)** — Autonomous QA engineer that verifies an implementation via real execution (Playwright, screenshots, console/network checks) and runs a bounded repair loop until it passes.
- **[roast](roast/SKILL.MD)** — Stress-tests a business/product idea through an adversarial five-persona council and issues a GREEN LIGHT / RESHAPE / KILL verdict.
- **[production-grade](production-grade/SKILL.MD)** — Principal-engineer persona that generates production-ready code: secure, leak-free, observable, performant, and conceptually clear.
- **[spec-cleanup](spec-cleanup/SKILL.MD)** — Final-pass cleanup that rewrites a multi-draft spec/design doc into a clean, standalone specification.
- **[bank-statement-analyzer](finance/personal_finance/bank-statement-analyzer/SKILL.MD)** — Extracts, categorizes, and analyzes bank statement transactions into a privacy-safe cash flow report.
- **[credit-card-statement-analyzer](finance/personal_finance/credit-card-statement-analyzer/SKILL.MD)** — Analyzes credit card statements for spending, utilization, fees, rewards, and payment behavior.
- **[expenditure-synthesizer](finance/personal_finance/expenditure-synthesizer/SKILL.MD)** — Combines bank and credit card analyses into one reconciled, double-count-free financial report.
- **[investment-thesis-red-team](finance/investment/investment-thesis-red-team/SKILL.MD)** — Attacks an investment thesis like a short seller: assumption audit, bear case, kill criteria, risk score.
- **[investment-bias-auditor](finance/investment/investment-bias-auditor/SKILL.MD)** — Detects behavioral biases in a trading journal with quoted evidence and a formula-based tilt score.
- **[forecast-calibration-tracker](finance/investment/forecast-calibration-tracker/SKILL.MD)** — Measures whether stated forecast confidence matches realized accuracy, with Brier scores and calibration buckets.
- **[investment-decision-reviewer](finance/investment/investment-decision-reviewer/SKILL.MD)** — Scores an investment decision's process independent of its outcome and classifies it on the skill/luck matrix.
- **[investment-memo-reviewer](finance/investment/investment-memo-reviewer/SKILL.MD)** — Grades an investment memo against an institutional committee rubric with per-section grades.
- **[management-credibility-tracker](finance/investment/management-credibility-tracker/SKILL.MD)** — Builds a promise-vs-delivery ledger from management communications and scores credibility over time.
- **[capital-allocation-auditor](finance/investment/capital-allocation-auditor/SKILL.MD)** — Grades management's capital allocation (capex, M&A, buybacks, dividends, debt) dollar-weighted by deployment.
- **[company-narrative-auditor](finance/investment/company-narrative-auditor/SKILL.MD)** — Tests company claims against tiered external evidence and reports corroborations and contradictions.
- **[thesis-evolution-tracker](finance/investment/thesis-evolution-tracker/SKILL.MD)** — Diffs thesis versions over time and distinguishes healthy evolution from thesis creep.
- **[investment-postmortem-analyzer](finance/investment/investment-postmortem-analyzer/SKILL.MD)** — Reviews completed trades against their theses, attributes results to skill vs luck, and extracts repeatable lessons.
